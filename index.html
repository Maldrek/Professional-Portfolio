About Me

I am an ambitious Full-Stack Developer looking to help create innovative and boundary-breaking ways to develop the web. My theatrical upbringing along with my technical skills help me to take on any client and bring a unique perspective and mindset to my projects. I am currently looking to join a team of like-minded entrepreneurs and firms who seek to provide something new and creative to the people they work with.

Born and raised in Kansas, I have had the opportunity to travel all over the world and meet people from all aspects of life. I have attended international leadership conferences that have helped me to hone my organizational and time-management skills, and have won top speaking awards and have had the opportunity to coach high-schoolers from all across the nation. This has helped shape my creative outlook and has allowed me to learn how to interact with many different kinds of people. If you are familiar with the DISC personality system, that is something that I work hard to abide by when it comes to communicating with clients as well as my peeers.

When I am not at my computer, you can probably find me in rehearsals for a show or binge-watching Black Mirror on Netflix. I bowl a mean 100 on a good day, and my knowledge of anime is expansive. Other hobbies include reading (current book is "Think and Grow Rich" by Napoleon Hill), writing, singing showtunes, and watching "Last Week Tonight" with John Oliver on YouTube (because who can afford HBO anymore?).
